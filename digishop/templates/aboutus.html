Founded in 2021 by Anthony & TheFreeThinker. We are a digital marketplace for NFT's (Non-fungible token). Our major focus is to provide users with a 3rd-party marketplace catered to the community in efforts of making transactions secured and effortless.

We are not affiliated with NBATopShot

How It Works

This is a marketplace similar to many others. How it works is simple.


For Sellers:

  1. You send your moments to our accounts and set the price you want.
  2. Wait until your moment sells on our marketplace. Once it sells, the funds will be available in your account wallet.
  3. Withdraw your funds to your PayPal account. All withdraw requests are manually reviewed to ensure the safety of the marketplace. Any withdraws are subject to up to 7-10 days in process.

For Buyers:

  1. Find the moment you want and the select any listing available.
  2. Go through the PayPal purchase procedure. After submitting your purchase, your moment will be queued for withdraw. Due to the nature of these moments all sales are final, except in rare cases.
  3. All withdraw requests are manually reviewed to ensure the safety of the marketplace. Any withdraws are subject to up to 7-10 days in process.
